Inside Out

 Anger personality is an often reaction for us whenever we feel frustrated. It’s like we are suffering in our inner self that we take it out in a bad way. Anger has a fiery spirit that gets mad easily when things don't go as planned.
Sadness personality is a common reaction to us that people misunderstood that being sad isn’t a negative or bad emotion. This emotion is our gentle self that it’s okay to feel sluggish or being emotional.
Joy personality is a joy or lightness in your body. Wherein you will feel happy, vivacious, preppy, optimistic, and a cheerful attitude. It’s like you can’t help but smile when things have been good. You live your life to the fullest with your smile :)
Disgust personality is where we are being stubborn, annoyed, and disgusted with someone or to ourselves. It’s like we tend to judge people that sometimes disgust can be negative. How Disgust means a feeling that something has made us unpleasant or offensive.
Fear personality is where it shows our weaknesses. How we have our own fears that sometimes fear took us over without even thinking. When danger comes, fear always comes into our minds and wants to avoid what’s happening.
